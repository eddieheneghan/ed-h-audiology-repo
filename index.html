Preparing for Your Child's Hearing Test

A few simple steps at home can help us get the most accurate results for your child’s hearing.

Early Years

6 Months to 3 Years

Visual Reinforcement Audiometry (VRA)

We use animated toys and sounds to turn the test into a fun game. We observe your child's natural response to different pitches.

  • Ear Play: Gently touch your child's ears during play to help them get used to the sensation.
  • Headphone Intro: If you have earmuffs or headphones, let them wear them for a few seconds.
  • Best Time: Try to ensure they aren't due for a nap or a meal during the appointment time.

Preschool & School Age

3 Years and Onwards

Play Audiometry

Your child will be taught to perform a simple task (like moving a block) every time they hear a sound through headphones.

  • Listening Games: Practice "Stop and Go" games where they wait for your signal to move a toy.
  • Superhero Check: Tell them they are going to play a special superhero hearing game.
  • Reward: Plan a small, positive activity for after the appointment to build confidence.
